## 6. REVISION POLICY

### 6.1 Included Revisions
Each deliverable includes up to **[X] rounds of revisions** based on consolidated client feedback.

### 6.2 Revision Round Defined
A revision round consists of:
- Feedback submitted within [X] business days of deliverable presentation
- All stakeholder feedback consolidated into single, unified direction
- Changes that refine the approved creative direction

### 6.3 Additional Revisions
Revision requests beyond included rounds will be quoted as change orders per Section 8.

### 6.4 What Constitutes New Work
The following are NOT revisions and will be treated as change orders:
- Requests that depart from approved creative direction
- Expansion of deliverable scope
- Feedback submitted after written approval of a deliverable
- Requests for deliverables not included in Section 2

---

## 7. ACCEPTANCE & APPROVAL

### 7.1 Deliverable Presentation

### 7.2 Feedback Submission
All revision feedback must be submitted through designated feedback platform to ensure proper version tracking.

### 7.3 Review Period
Client has [X] business days from deliverable presentation to:
- Submit approval to proceed, or
- Submit consolidated revision feedback

### 7.4 Deemed Acceptance
If Client does not respond within [X] business days of deliverable presentation, the deliverable is deemed approved.

### 7.5 Final Acceptance
Project is complete upon:
- Client's written approval of all deliverables, or
- Deemed acceptance per Section 7.4, and
- Delivery of all final files and documentation

---

## 8. CHANGE ORDERS

### 8.1 Scope Changes
Any work outside scope defined in Section 2, or revisions beyond those included in Section 6, requires a written change order.

### 8.2 Change Order Process
1. Client submits change request in writing
2. ProCreativ provides written quote including scope, timeline impact, and cost within [X] business days
3. Client approves change order in writing before work begins
4. Change order fees due upon completion of change order work

### 8.3 Timeline Impact
Approved change orders may extend project timeline. ProCreativ will communicate revised timeline with each change order quote.

## Common Sections

These sections can be added to any engagement type as needed:

### NDA/Confidentiality Addendum
Use when project requires enhanced confidentiality (e.g., pre-launch products, M&A materials).

### Non-Compete Provision
Use when client requests exclusivity within their industry for engagement duration.

### Subcontractor Disclosure
Use when ProCreativ may engage subcontractors for specialized work.
