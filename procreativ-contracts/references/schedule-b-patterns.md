## Fixed-Scope Structure

```markdown
# Schedule B: Pricing & Payment Terms

THIS SCHEDULE B is made as of _______________ (the "Effective Date") by and between 
**ProCreativ Solutions LLC** and **[CLIENT NAME]** ("Client").

This Schedule B is incorporated into and made part of the Master Service Agreement between the parties.

## 1. PROJECT FEE

### 1.1 Total Project Investment

| Description | Amount |
|-------------|--------|
| [Project Name] | $[AMOUNT] USD |

This is a fixed-price engagement for the scope defined in Schedule A.

### 1.2 What's Included
The Project Fee covers:
- All deliverables specified in Schedule A, Section 2
- Up to [X] rounds of revisions per deliverable
- Project management and communication
- Final file delivery and developer handoff documentation

---

## 2. PAYMENT SCHEDULE

### 2.1 Payment Milestones

| Milestone | Amount | Due Date |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| **Deposit** | $[AMOUNT] ([X]%) | Upon signing this Agreement |
| **Final Payment** | $[AMOUNT] ([Y]%) | [24 hours before final deliverable handoff / Upon project completion] |

### 2.2 Deposit
- Deposit is required to commence work
- Work will not begin until Deposit is received
- Deposit is non-refundable once work has commenced, except as provided in Section 6

### 2.3 Final Payment
- ProCreativ will notify Client when deliverables are ready for final handoff
- Client shall remit Final Payment within [X] hours/days of notification
- Final deliverables and source files transferred upon receipt of Final Payment

### 2.4 Payment Due Dates
All payments due on dates specified above. If due date falls on weekend or U.S. federal holiday, payment due next business day.

---

## 3. PAYMENT METHODS

### 3.1 Accepted Methods
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- ACH bank transfer
- Debit card
- Wire transfer (additional fees may apply)

### 3.2 Payment Processing
- Invoices sent via Stripe
- Client may pay directly through invoice link
- ACH and wire transfer details provided upon request

---

## 4. LATE PAYMENTS

### 4.1 Interest
Late payments incur interest at 1.5% per month (or maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower), calculated from original due date.

### 4.2 Suspension of Services
- If payment not received within [X] business days of due date, ProCreativ may suspend deliverable handoff until payment received
- ProCreativ will provide written notice before suspension
- Project timeline extended by duration of any payment-related delays

### 4.3 Collection Costs
Client agrees to pay all reasonable costs of collection, including attorneys' fees, incurred by ProCreativ in collecting overdue amounts.

---

## 5. CHANGE ORDERS

### 5.1 Additional Work
Work outside scope defined in Schedule A will be quoted separately as change order.

### 5.2 Change Order Pricing
Change orders priced based on:
- Scope and complexity of additional work
- Impact on project timeline
- Current availability

### 5.3 Change Order Payment Terms
- Change orders under $[X]: Due upon completion of change order work
- Change orders $[X] and above: [X]% due upon approval, [Y]% due upon completion

### 5.4 Hourly Rate for Minor Additions
For small additions not warranting formal change order, ProCreativ's hourly rate is **$[RATE]/hour**, billed in [X]-minute increments. Client will be notified prior to any hourly work being performed.

---

## 6. REFUND POLICY

### 6.1 Before Work Commences
If Client terminates before ProCreativ has commenced work, Deposit refunded in full within [X] business days.

### 6.2 After Work Commences
If Client terminates after work has commenced:
- Deposit is non-refundable
- Client pays for all work completed through termination date
- If work completed exceeds Deposit amount, Client pays difference
- If work completed is less than Deposit amount, no additional refund provided

### 6.3 Termination by ProCreativ
If ProCreativ terminates (other than for Client's breach or non-payment):
- Client pays for all work completed through termination date
- ProCreativ refunds any prepaid amounts for work not performed
- ProCreativ delivers all completed work product for which payment received

### 6.4 Termination for Client Breach
If ProCreativ terminates due to Client's material breach or non-payment:
- All amounts paid are non-refundable
- All outstanding amounts become immediately due
- ProCreativ may withhold delivery of work product until all amounts paid

---

## 7. TAXES

### 7.1 Exclusive of Taxes
All fees stated in this Schedule B are exclusive of applicable taxes.

### 7.2 Client Responsibility
Client responsible for all applicable local, state, and federal taxes imposed upon Services, including sales tax, use tax, and VAT, except taxes based on ProCreativ's income.

### 7.3 Tax Collection
ProCreativ will collect and remit sales tax where required by law. Tax amounts itemized on invoices.

### 7.4 Tax Exemption
If Client is tax-exempt, Client must provide valid tax exemption certificate before first invoice issued.

---

## 8. PAYMENT TERMS MODIFICATIONS

### 8.1 Amendments
This Schedule B may only be modified by written agreement signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

### 8.2 No Verbal Modifications
No verbal agreements or representations regarding payment terms are binding unless documented in writing and signed by both parties.

---

## 9. SUMMARY

| Item | Amount | Note |
|------|--------|------|
| **Total Project Fee** | $[AMOUNT] | — |
| **Deposit** | $[AMOUNT] | *(due upon signing)* |
| **Final Payment** | $[AMOUNT] | *(due [timing])* |
| **Change Order Hourly Rate** | $[RATE]/hr | *(notified upfront before billing)* |

---

*This Schedule B is incorporated into and governed by the Master Service Agreement between the parties.*
```

For larger projects with multiple milestones:

```markdown
## 2. PAYMENT SCHEDULE

| Milestone | Amount | Due Date | Trigger |
|-----------|--------|----------|---------|
| **Deposit** | $[AMOUNT] ([X]%) | Upon signing | Agreement execution |
| **Milestone 1** | $[AMOUNT] ([Y]%) | [Date/Week X] | [Deliverable approval] |
| **Milestone 2** | $[AMOUNT] ([Z]%) | [Date/Week X] | [Deliverable approval] |
| **Final Payment** | $[AMOUNT] ([W]%) | Project completion | All deliverables approved |
```

## Retainer Structure

```markdown
# Schedule B: Pricing & Payment Terms

THIS SCHEDULE B is made as of _______________ (the "Effective Date") by and between 
**ProCreativ Solutions LLC** and **[CLIENT NAME]** ("Client").

This Schedule B is incorporated into and made part of the Master Service Agreement between the parties.

## 1. SUBSCRIPTION PRICING

### [Service Tier Name]

**Monthly Subscription:** $[AMOUNT]

Includes:
- [Included item 1]
- [Included item 2]
- [Included item 3]
- Source files included with all deliverables

---

## 2. BILLING DETAILS

### 2.1 Payment Schedule
- **Billing Cycle:** Monthly, in advance
- **First Payment:** Due upon agreement signing
- **Recurring Payments:** Same calendar day each month
- **Payment Method:** Auto-charge via Stripe

### 2.2 Custom Payment Terms
- Standard terms above apply unless specific payment terms otherwise defined
- Any custom payment arrangements must be documented in writing
- Custom terms may include: annual billing, net terms, or alternate schedules

### 2.3 Accepted Payment Methods
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- ACH bank transfer (upon request)
- Debit card
- Wire transfer (additional fees may apply)

### 2.4 Proration & Mid-Cycle Adjustments
Subscription fees for partial billing periods calculated by multiplying daily rate (Monthly Fee ÷ 30) by number of calendar days of service. Same daily rate applies to mid-cycle upgrades or downgrades; resulting charges or credits appear on next invoice.

---

## 3. LATE PAYMENT PENALTIES

Reinstatement requires payment of all outstanding amounts plus any interest accrued under MSA §3.3.

---

## 4. ADDITIONAL SERVICES

Services outside subscription scope:

### 4.1 Rush Requests
- **Rate:** $[AMOUNT] per request
- **Defined as:** Any Simple or Standard request requiring delivery within [X] business hours
- **Timeline measured from:** ProCreativ's confirmation of request receipt during business hours
- Complex Requests cannot be rushed
- Subject to availability; ProCreativ reserves right to decline

### 4.2 Weekend Requests
- **Rate:** $[AMOUNT] per request ([X]x rush rate)
- For work performed on Saturday or Sunday
- Standard turnaround times apply unless also marked as rush
- **Combining weekend + rush:** $[AMOUNT]
- Subject to availability; ProCreativ reserves right to decline

---

## 5. RESERVED

---

## 6. PRICING ADJUSTMENTS

### 6.1 Review Schedule
- Rates reviewed every [X] days
- Based on: service utilization, request complexity, market conditions, operational costs

### 6.2 Change Notification
- Any proposed increase requires mutual written agreement per MSA §10.7
- ProCreativ will provide proposed rates at least [X] days in advance for Client review
- Option to cancel without penalty before increase takes effect
- No retroactive pricing changes

---

## 7. REFUND POLICY

### 7.1 General Policy
- Except as otherwise set forth in Agreement, NO REFUNDS offered after initial [X]-day period
- All sales final after satisfaction guarantee period
- No credits for unused services

### 7.2 Satisfaction Guarantee
- First [X] days only: Full refund if unsatisfied
- Request must be in writing
- Refund processed within [X] business days
- All work produced must be returned/destroyed

### 7.3 Service Credits
- Issued for service failures at ProCreativ's discretion
- Cannot exceed monthly subscription amount
- Applied to future invoices only
- No cash value

---

## 8. TAXES

- Prices exclude applicable taxes
- Client responsible for all applicable taxes
- ProCreativ will collect sales tax where required by law
- Tax-exempt clients must provide valid exemption certificate

---

## 9. PAYMENT TERMS MODIFICATIONS

- This Schedule B requires mutual written agreement to modify
- Client may terminate rather than accept pricing changes
- All payment obligations incurred remain enforceable

---

*This Schedule B is incorporated into and governed by the Master Service Agreement between the parties.*
```
