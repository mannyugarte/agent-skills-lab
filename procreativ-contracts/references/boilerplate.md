## Revision Policies

### Standard (2 Rounds)
```markdown
## REVISION POLICY

### Included Revisions
Each deliverable includes up to **two (2) rounds of revisions** based on consolidated client feedback.

### Revision Round Defined
A revision round consists of:
- Feedback submitted within two (2) business days of deliverable presentation
- All stakeholder feedback consolidated into single, unified direction
- Changes that refine the approved creative direction

### Additional Revisions
Revision requests beyond included rounds will be quoted as change orders.

### What Constitutes New Work
The following are NOT revisions and will be treated as change orders:
- Requests that depart from approved creative direction
- Expansion of deliverable scope
- Feedback submitted after written approval of a deliverable
- Requests for deliverables not included in scope
```

### Unlimited Revisions (Retainer)
```markdown
## REVISION PROCESS

### Unlimited Revisions
- All requests include unlimited revisions
```

## Acceptance Terms

### Standard Acceptance
```markdown
## ACCEPTANCE & APPROVAL

### Deliverable Presentation
ProCreativ will present deliverables via Figma with walkthrough as needed (live or recorded).

### Feedback Submission
All revision feedback must be submitted through designated feedback platform to ensure proper version tracking.

### Review Period
Client has two (2) business days from deliverable presentation to:
- Submit approval to proceed, or
- Submit consolidated revision feedback

### Deemed Acceptance
If Client does not respond within five (5) business days of deliverable presentation, the deliverable is deemed approved.

### Final Acceptance
Project is complete upon:
- Client's written approval of all deliverables, or
- Deemed acceptance per above, and
- Delivery of all final files and documentation
```

## Termination Clauses

### Fixed-Scope Termination
```markdown
## TERMINATION

### Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate this engagement for convenience by providing seven (7) calendar days' written notice.

### Effect of Early Termination
Upon early termination:
- Client pays for all work completed through termination date
- ProCreativ delivers all completed work product for which payment has been received
- Refund of prepaid fees, if any, determined per Schedule B
```

### Retainer Termination
```markdown
## TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE

Either Party may terminate the Services for convenience by giving the other Party thirty (30) calendar days' written notice.
```

### Hourly Termination
```markdown
## TERMINATION

Either party may terminate with fourteen (14) calendar days' written notice. Upon termination:
- Client pays for all hours worked through termination date
- ProCreativ delivers all completed work for which payment received
- Unused deposit balance refunded within ten (10) business days
```
