# MSA Reference

This file contains the ProCreativ Master Service Agreement structure for reference only. **Do not output this content** — it's for understanding the legal framework that Schedule A and B plug into.

## MSA Overview

The MSA is the foundational legal document that governs all client engagements. It covers:

1. **Definitions & Interpretation** — How terms are defined, schedule hierarchy
2. **Services Framework** — Service standards, client cooperation requirements
3. **Payment Terms** — References Schedule B, covers failed/late payments, taxes
4. **Intellectual Property Rights** — Work-for-hire, rights transfer, portfolio rights
5. **Confidentiality** — 3-year obligation, exceptions, return of information
6. **Representations & Warranties** — Mutual reps, ProCreativ warranties, disclaimers
7. **Indemnification** — Client and ProCreativ indemnification obligations
8. **Limitation of Liability** — Cap at fees paid in 6 months, max $500K aggregate
9. **Term & Termination** — Termination triggers, effect, survival provisions
10. **General Provisions** — Independent contractor, force majeure, governing law (Delaware), arbitration (Missouri), amendments, notices

## Key Provisions to Remember

### Payment Terms (§3)
- Schedule B controls specific pricing
- Failed payments: Stripe smart retries, 10-day cure period
- Late payments: 1.5%/month interest
- Client responsible for taxes (except ProCreativ income taxes)

### IP Rights (§4)
- All deliverables are work-for-hire, owned by Client upon full payment
- ProCreativ retains pre-existing materials, tools, methodologies
- Portfolio display allowed unless Client requests confidentiality in writing

### Confidentiality (§5)
- 3-year obligation post-termination
- Standard exceptions (public info, prior knowledge, independent development, legal requirement)

### Liability (§8)
- Cap: Total fees paid in 6 months preceding claim
- Absolute cap: $500,000
- No consequential, indirect, punitive damages

### Termination (§9)
- For convenience: Per Schedule A notice period
- For breach: 10-day cure period
- For non-payment: Immediate after 10-day notice
- Effect: Pay for work done, deliver paid deliverables, return confidential info

### Governing Law & Disputes (§10.4-10.5)
- Delaware law governs
- Binding arbitration under AAA Commercial Rules in Missouri

## What Schedule A Must Include

Schedule A is referenced by MSA §2.1 and must define:
- Specific services to be provided
- Deliverables
- Timelines
- Client cooperation requirements
- Feedback timeframes
- Termination notice period for convenience

## What Schedule B Must Include

Schedule B is referenced by MSA §3.1 and must define:
- All fees
- Payment schedule and methods
- Refund policy
- Change order pricing
- Any proration rules

## Amendment Requirements (§10.7)

- MSA: Written, signed by both parties
- Schedule A: Mutual consent, written, signed by both parties
- Schedule B: Written agreement signed by both parties

## Notices (§10.9)

All notices in writing to:
- Client: Email in signature page + legal contact email
- ProCreativ: support@procreativ.solutions
