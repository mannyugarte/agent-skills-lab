# Retainer Example: ProCreativ Partnership

This example demonstrates the structure and tone for an ongoing retainer/subscription engagement.

## Schedule A: Scope of Work & Definitions

THIS SCHEDULE A is made as of _______________ (the "Effective Date") by and between **ProCreativ Solutions LLC** and **[CLIENT NAME]** ("Client").

This Schedule A is incorporated into and made part of the Master Service Agreement between the parties.

### 1. SERVICE MODEL

ProCreativ Partnership provides unlimited design requests on a monthly subscription basis, allowing flexible, ongoing creative support without per-project pricing. All services and deliverables are subject to Client's approval, in its sole discretion.

- Design consultation (up to 2 hours/month)
- Implementation guidance

- Brand system documentation

---

### 3. "UNLIMITED" DEFINITION

"Unlimited requests" means:
- You may submit as many design requests as needed via our intake form
- We work on one request at a time in order received
- You may queue additional requests while we work on the active request
- No monthly cap on total requests or revisions
- Each request should be a discrete, defined project
  - Good request: "Design a 10-slide sales deck" ‚úÖ
  - Too broad: "Redesign all our marketing materials" ‚ùå
- Complex projects may be broken into multiple requests

---

### 4. EXCLUDED SERVICES

The following are NOT included in the subscription:
- Website development, coding, or programming
- Video editing or animation
- 3D rendering or modeling
- Physical printing or production costs
- Paid stock photography or premium font licenses
- Third-party software licenses
- Copywriting (beyond design placeholder text)
- Photography or photoshoots
- Rush delivery outside business hours (available for additional fee)
- Strategy consulting exceeding 2 hours per month

---

### 5. SERVICE LEVELS & TURNAROUND TIMES

#### 5.1 Turnaround by Complexity

**Simple Requests (24-48 business hours)**
- Social media graphics
- Basic image edits
- Simple layout adjustments
- Icon or graphic updates
- Color/font changes to existing designs

**Standard Requests (48-72 business hours)**
- Presentation design (up to 20 slides)
- One-pagers and fact sheets
- Email templates
- Digital ads (standard sizes)
- Marketing collateral updates

**Complex Requests (3-7 business days)**
- Brand identity development
- Large presentations (20+ slides)
- Multi-page documents (eBooks, reports)
- Campaign concepts
- Custom illustrations
- Complete design systems

#### 5.2 Revision Turnaround
- Minor revisions: Within 24 business hours
- Major revisions: 24-48 business hours
- Urgent revisions: Best effort basis

---

### 6. BUSINESS HOURS & AVAILABILITY

#### 6.1 Standard Business Hours
- Monday ‚Äì Friday: 9:00 AM ‚Äì 5:00 PM Central Time
- Holidays: Closed on federal holidays and company holidays
- Weekends: Not included in standard service

#### 6.2 Response Times
- Initial request acknowledgment: Within 4 business hours
- Questions during active projects: Within 4 business hours
- General inquiries: Within 1 business day

#### 6.3 Turnaround Calculation
- Timer begins upon ProCreativ's confirmation of request receipt
- If clarification needed, timer pauses until Client response
- We'll notify you if a request will exceed standard timeline

---

### 7. REQUEST SUBMISSION PROCESS

#### 7.1 How to Submit Requests
- Use designated request form or platform
- Include all relevant details:
  - Project description
  - Deliverable specifications
  - Brand guidelines or assets
  - Examples or inspiration
  - Desired timeline (if different from standard)

#### 7.2 Request Prioritization
- Requests processed first-in, first-out
- Client may reprioritize queued requests
- Rush requests subject to availability and additional fees

---

### 8. REVISION PROCESS

#### 8.1 Unlimited Revisions
- All requests include unlimited revisions
- Revisions must relate to original request scope
- Best practice: Consolidate feedback for efficiency

#### 8.2 Scope Changes
Significant departures from original request may be treated as new requests:
- **Revision:** "Make the logo bigger" ‚úÖ

### 9. COMMUNICATION & COLLABORATION

#### 9.1 Communication Channels
- Primary: Slack, Teams, or email (as agreed)
- Request submission: Designated form
- File sharing: Google Drive, Dropbox, or agreed platform

#### 9.2 Quality Standards
- Professional, polished deliverables
- Adherence to provided brand guidelines
- Error-free final files
- Appropriate file formats for intended use

---

### 10. RUSH REQUESTS

Rush requests (faster than standard turnaround or outside business hours):
- Subject to current workload and availability
- Additional fees apply (see Schedule B)
- Best effort basis ‚Äì not guaranteed
- Advance notice increases accommodation likelihood

---

### 11. TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE

Either Party may terminate the Services for convenience by giving the other Party thirty (30) calendar days' written notice.

### 4. ADDITIONAL SERVICES

Services outside subscription scope:

#### 4.1 Rush Requests
- **Rate:** $1,500 per request
- **Defined as:** Any Simple or Standard request (per Schedule A) requiring delivery within 24 business hours
- **Timeline measured from:** ProCreativ's confirmation of request receipt during business hours
- Requests received outside business hours acknowledged at start of next business day
- Business hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM ‚Äì 5:00 PM Central Time (excluding weekends and holidays)
- All timelines calculated using Central Time
  - Example: Friday 8am request due Monday 8am = Rush request only (only 1 business day available, no weekend work required)
- Holidays: U.S. federal holidays plus any dates ProCreativ designates as company holidays with 30 days advance notice
- Complex Requests (per Schedule A) cannot be rushed
- Rush determination: ProCreativ will confirm rush status and associated fee with Client before proceeding if timeline qualifies as rush request
- Good faith exceptions may be made for time zone accommodations at ProCreativ's discretion
- Subject to availability; ProCreativ reserves right to decline

#### 4.2 Weekend Requests
- **Rate:** $2,250 per request (1.5x rush request rate)
- For work performed on Saturday or Sunday
- Standard turnaround times apply unless also marked as rush
- **Combining weekend + rush request:** $3,750
  - Example: Friday 8am request due Saturday 5pm = Rush + Weekend request ($3,750)
- Rush and weekend request determination: ProCreativ will confirm status and associated fee with Client before proceeding
- Good faith exceptions may be made for time zone accommodations at ProCreativ's discretion
- Subject to availability; ProCreativ reserves right to decline
